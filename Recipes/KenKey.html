Kenkey

Kenkey with Fish

Description

Also called Nkran Dokono or Accra Kenkey, the Ga Kenkey is most of the times sold at night along the streets.

Ga Kenkey is a popular carbohydrate food in Ghana. It is mostly served with pepper sauce, shito and fried fish or tilapia.

Ingrdients

  • corn dough
  • corn hust
  • salt
  • water

Steps in Cooking Kenkey

  1. Put half of the corn dough in a bowl and mix with water
  2. Boil corn dough until half way cooked
  3. Spread the dough on a large clean saucepan and add the remaining raw corn dough using a wooden
    ladle.
  4. Mix together thoroughly.
  5. Mould into preferred pieces/size.
  6. Wrap each with the corn husks
  7. Placed some corn husks at the bottom of a big saucepan and arrange the wrapped dough nicely on it.
  8. Add enough water, cover and boil for about 10 minutes.
  9. Kenkey is ready.
  10. erve with hot pepper sauce, chilli sauce, shito, stew or soup
